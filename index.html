The Development
East Cape is what the rest of Los Cabos was 25 years ago.

Raw, undeveloped, and on the cusp of a transformation. Mar de Plata sits just 600 meters from Shipwreck Beach, one of Baja’s most revered surf breaks, with panoramic views of the Sea of Cortez and the Sierra de la Laguna mountains.

This is pre-infrastructure land at a fraction of the price of the Los Cabos Corridor. Early buyers here are positioning in one of Mexico’s last accessible coastal frontiers before the road paves, electricity arrives, and values correct upward permanently.

El Cabo Este es lo que el corredor turístico era hace 25 años — virgen, auténtico, y en vísperas de una transformación.

Find Us

Mar de Plata is located on the East Cape of Baja California Sur, along the Camino Cabo Este — the coastal road that connects the East Cape’s pristine beaches from San José del Cabo northward to the Sea of Cortez.

The development sits 600 meters from Shipwreck Beach, with Cabo Pulmo’s UNESCO marine reserve to the north and the Four Seasons Costa Palmas golf resort just 30 minutes up the coast.
